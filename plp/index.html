Healthy Habits for a Better Life

Simple daily practices for a healthier body and mind.

1. Eat Nutritious Foods

A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins supports overall health. Drink plenty of water and limit processed foods and added sugars.

2. Stay Physically Active

Regular exercise improves heart health, boosts mood, and helps manage weight. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate activity most days of the week.

3. Get Quality Sleep

Adults should get 7–9 hours of sleep per night. Maintain a consistent bedtime, reduce screen time before bed, and create a relaxing sleep environment.

4. Take Care of Your Mental Health

Practice Mindfulness

Meditation, journaling, and mindful breathing can reduce stress and improve focus.

Connect with Others

Positive relationships and social support are key to emotional well-being.
